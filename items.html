Here are the makeup products we sell

Concelar

A concealer or color corrector is a type of cosmetic that is used to mask imperfections on the skin.

Mascara

A cosmetic substance for darkening, lengthening, curling, coloring, and thickening the eyelashes, applied with a brush or rod.

Lipstick

Is a waxy, slightly creamy substance in a stick that's tinted with red pigment that colors your lips.

Powder

Are products that are intended to change the appearance of facial skin.

Eyeshadow

Is a cosmetic applied primarily to the eyelids to attract attention to the wearer's eyes, making them stand out or look more attractive.
